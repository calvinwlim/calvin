ENV|Spilled Ink

Stemming from an initial, self-taught approach to graphic and motion design, ENV|Spilled Ink was born out of a desire for personal authenticity and creativity in design. Our disruptive, hybrid attitude shines through into every venture, having it act as a business, diary, portfolio, and more. Located in the Bay Area, CA, the studio is operated by its founder, Calvin Lim.
